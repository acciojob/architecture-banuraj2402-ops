Exploring Key Architectural Eras

Classical Architecture

Dating back to ancient Greece and Rome, this style is characterized by its emphasis onsymmetry, proportion, and order. Key elements include the use of columns (Doric, Ionic, Corinthian), pediments, and elaborate friezes.

Gothic Architecture

Emerging in the Middle Ages, Gothic style pushed boundaries with its revolutionary engineering. It is defined by the pointed arch, the ribbed vault, and the flying buttress. These structural elements allowed for immense height and the integration of large stained-glass windows.
